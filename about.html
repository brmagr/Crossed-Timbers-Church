About Crossed Timbers Church


Crossed Timbers Church was formed to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ where God may lead us.
His Word, His Way describes our attitudes in everything we do.
As a side note, Crossed Timbers Church was formed in 2012 by God's calling to four couples.
His Word, His Way guides our congregation's journey to "...make disciples of all nations".
